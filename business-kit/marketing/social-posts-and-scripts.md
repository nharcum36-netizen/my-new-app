# Social Posts and Scripts

## Post Script 1 - Natural English Phrases

Hook: Stop sounding like a textbook.

Script:
- Say: I am running late.
- Not: I will be late.
- Say: That makes sense.
- Not: I understand your logic.
- Say: Can you repeat that?
- Not: Please say again.

## Post Script 2 - Grammar Quick Fix

Hook: One grammar fix that instantly improves your English.

Script:
- Incorrect: I am agree.
- Correct: I agree.
- Incorrect: She don’t like coffee.
- Correct: She doesn’t like coffee.
