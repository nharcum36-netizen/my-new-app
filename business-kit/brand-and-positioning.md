# Brand and Positioning

## Niche

Primary niche: Conversational English for adults (immigrants and international professionals).

Secondary niche: Interview and workplace communication coaching.

## Mission Statement

We help adult learners speak English confidently for work, daily life, and opportunity.

## Promise

Speak clearly and confidently in real-life situations within 8 weeks through practical speaking-first coaching.

## Teaching Style

- Conversation-first lessons
- Friendly correction without embarrassment
- Real-world role-play and repetition
- Weekly progress snapshots

## Core Selling Points

- Personalized speaking correction
- Real-life topics (meetings, interviews, travel, phone calls)
- Confidence-focused coaching framework
- Structured curriculum with measurable progress
