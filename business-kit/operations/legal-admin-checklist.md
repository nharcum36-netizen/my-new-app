# Legal and Admin Checklist

## Business Setup

- Register business name (based on local requirements)
- Open dedicated business bank account
- Separate business and personal expenses

## Tax and Accounting

- Track all income and expenses monthly
- Store invoices and receipts
- Confirm local tax filing obligations for tutoring income

## Policies

Publish these on website before launch:

- Cancellation policy (24-hour notice)
- Refund policy
- No-show policy
- Privacy policy
- Terms of service

## Data and Security

- Use strong passwords and 2FA for Stripe, email, and booking tools
- Limit admin access to payment and student systems
- Back up student records weekly
