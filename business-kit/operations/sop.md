# Operations SOP

## Weekly Schedule Template

- Tuesday to Friday: evening sessions
- Saturday: morning sessions and admin block
- Sunday: content scheduling and curriculum prep

## Booking Flow

1. Lead books trial in Calendly.
2. Confirmation email includes Zoom link and pre-lesson questionnaire.
3. Reminder automation at 24 hours and 1 hour before lesson.
4. Post-lesson follow-up sent within 12 hours.

## Student Tracking

Track in Notion or Google Sheets:

- Student goal
- CEFR level estimate
- Attendance rate
- Weekly confidence score (1 to 10)
- Vocabulary growth target
- Next milestone date

## Follow-Up Templates

### Trial Follow-Up (same day)

Thank you for today. You did well in [strength].

Your focus area this week: [target].

Recommended plan: [offer].

Book your next session here: [link].

### Weekly Progress Update

This week you improved in [area].

Next week we will focus on [area].

Homework: [task].

## KPI Review (every Sunday)

- Leads generated
- Trial lessons booked
- Trial to paid conversion rate
- Monthly recurring revenue
- Student retention rate
