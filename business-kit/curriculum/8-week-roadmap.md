# 8-Week Curriculum Roadmap

## Beginner Track

### Weeks 1-2
- Introductions and daily routine conversations
- Essential present tense patterns
- Confidence drills for short answers

### Weeks 3-4
- Shopping, travel, and appointment dialogues
- Question forms and clarification phrases
- Listening and shadowing practice

### Weeks 5-6
- Past experiences and storytelling basics
- Pronunciation focus: endings and stress
- Role-play conversations

### Weeks 7-8
- Workplace basics and phone conversations
- Extended speaking tasks
- Final confidence assessment and next-level plan

## Intermediate Track

### Weeks 1-2
- Opinion and explanation language
- Fluent linking words and transitions

### Weeks 3-4
- Workplace small talk and professional tone
- Meeting phrases and summarizing points

### Weeks 5-6
- Problem-solving conversations
- Email language and response structure

### Weeks 7-8
- Presentation mini-speeches
- Interview simulation and feedback cycle

## Advanced Track

### Weeks 1-2
- Nuanced persuasion and diplomatic language
- Precision vocabulary expansion

### Weeks 3-4
- High-level discussion and argument structure
- Real-time correction and reformulation drills

### Weeks 5-6
- Leadership communication in meetings
- Negotiation and conflict language

### Weeks 7-8
- Formal presentation polish
- Final performance benchmark and continuation plan
