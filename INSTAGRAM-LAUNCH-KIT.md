**Caption:**
```
🇺🇸 Cultural Tip:

In English-speaking countries, when someone says "How are you?" they usually DON'T want a long answer.

❌ "I'm not good, I had a terrible morning, my car broke down..."

✅ "I'm good, thanks! How about you?"

It's a GREETING, not a real question! 😅

Exception: Close friends and family DO want the real answer.
```

### POST 14: Grammar Hack
**Image:** "When to use THE" flowchart
**Caption:**
```
🤔 When do you use "THE"?

This confuses EVERYONE! Here's the simple rule:

✅ Use THE when:
- There's only one: "the sun", "the moon"
- You mentioned it before: "I saw a dog. THE dog was cute."
- It's specific: "the teacher from my school"

❌ Don't use THE when:
- Talking generally: "Dogs are cute" (all dogs)
- Most countries: "Japan" not "the Japan"
- Most streets: "Main Street" not "the Main Street"

Still confused? We can practice together! Link in bio 📧

#EnglishGrammar #TheArticle #ESL #GrammarTips #LearnEnglish
```
